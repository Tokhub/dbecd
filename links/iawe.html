Dataset Name: Indian data for Ambient Water and Electricity Sensing (iAWE)

Data characteristics Multivariate time series Type of building(s) Residential, Three story Date 2013
Type of attributes Real, Integer Number of buildings 1 Duration 73 days
Level of detail whole house, appliance/circuit level Location of buildings Delhi, India Sampling frequency 1 Hz

Access to the dataset

Each individual channel of the data can be downloaded separately in either SQL or CSV format from: http://iawe.github.io/

Data set description

iAWE data set contains whole and sub-metered electricity, gas, and water consumption data from a single house in Delhi, India. The data have been captured during 73 days of the year, with one day fully labelled data. 33 sensors have been deployed through the house to collect relevant data.

Dataset structure

From the repository an overview of the deployment of project is available. In which location of meters, metered devices and sensors are shown. Due to the frequent electricity outages in Delhi, as stated by project providers, a histogram of outage statistics is provided. Furthermore record of voltage fluctuation is presented. The data are obtainable either selectively respecting to meters and sensors or in whole. A presentation and paper on the project details including motivation behind it, building and measurement characteristics and unique features of the India grid is also available from the link.
