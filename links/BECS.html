Data set description

The CBECS data set includes energy related data on the US's commercial buildings. According to the definition buildings such as hospitals and schools have been considered as well. the data have been collected in two phases, one is the building survey and the second is energy supplier survey. this survey is conducted every few years. the first one at 1979 and the latest at 2012. demography and characteristics like income and age. It also contains information on climate zones as well as building characteristics, and energy resource types and household expenditure on energy.
