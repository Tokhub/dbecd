Data set description

This dataset is provided by Department for environment food and rural affairs (defra) . It contains electricity consumption of 26 homes during one year and 225 homes during one months, across England. Some aims of this data capturing program were identifying and characterising the electric appliances, understanding the pattern of their usage particularly in peak times, and capturing user’s habit data for some specific appliances.
