Dataset name: Pecan Street (also known as Wiki Energy)

Data characteristics Multivariate time series Type of building(s) Residential Date 2011
Type of attributes Real and integer numbers , string Number of buildings 200 houses Duration One week
Level of detail Whole building, Appliance level Location of buildings Austin, US Sampling frequency Minutely

Access to the dataset

Making an application is needed for getting the data. The data are available to public in four levels of Commercial, academic, educator and registered user. Fees applies for some extend of access. Full details about accessing to the data is described here: http://www.pecanstreet.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Pecan-Street-Curated-datasets-overview.pdf

Data set description

Pecan street dataset has residential energy consumption data and includes a wide range of energy related devices including solar and electric vehicles. The data are provided in different formats including csv and xls upon the request.
